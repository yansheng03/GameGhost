Why does Game Ghost only sell hard copies?
Game Ghost exclusively sells physical hard copies of games as a deliberate choice catering to collectors and enthusiasts who value tangible, rare artifacts. This decision aligns with the platform's dedication to offering games that are hard to find in the digital era, contributing to a sense of exclusivity and scarcity. The emphasis on hard copies serves as a tribute to collectors, allowing them to showcase their curated collections with pride. Game Ghost recognizes the limited availability of certain titles and aims to preserve gaming history by offering physical releases. The decision is rooted in enhancing the ownership experience, providing gamers with a tangible sense of possession and connection to their games. The physical copies contribute to historical preservation within the gaming community, capturing specific moments in the industry's evolution. Game Ghost acknowledges and celebrates the nostalgia and sentimentality associated with physical copies, fostering a community that cherishes these aspects of gaming. The platform's commitment to hard copies goes beyond commerce, offering a unique and immersive gaming experience that goes beyond the digital realm.
